Final Project

Welcome to my Final Project Page!

This is a Madlibs game my partner Cade and I coded using our skills we learned during CSC 106. This game is a fun quick way to get a laugh out of a silly story! To get started, either quit reading up here and jump right into it, or keep reading on and I'll explain here! This game has a pre-determined paragraph that takes user inputs and passes them into the paragraph and returns it to them as a story. So just hit start, and start picking the words from the list you find the funniest, and just like that, the program will process it and send back your story! Didn't think it was funny enough? Hit "Try Again!" and give it another go with some even funnier words!
